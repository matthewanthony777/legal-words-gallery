In today's digital economy, protecting intellectual property has become more crucial than ever. This guide explores the fundamentals and recent developments.

### Patents
- Innovation protection
- Filing requirements

### Trademarks
- Brand protection

- Enforcement strategies

### Copyrights
- Creative works
- Digital rights
