# Understanding Intellectual Property Rights

In today's digital age, protecting intellectual property has become more crucial than ever. This guide explores the fundamental concepts and practical steps for safeguarding your intellectual assets.

## Types of Intellectual Property Protection

### Patents
Patents protect inventions and new technologies. Key aspects include:
- Utility patents
- Design patents
- Plant patents

### Trademarks
Trademarks protect brands and logos, including:
- Word marks
- Design marks
- Service marks

### Copyrights
Copyright protection covers creative works such as:
- Literary works
- Musical compositions
- Software code
- Artistic creations
